Step 1: Open tiktok.com in a new tab and make sure you're logged in to your account.

Step 2: Come back here and click the button below to start the process.

  • The extension will open your TikTok profile
  • Enter the "Reposts" tab
  • Open each reposted video
  • Remove it from your reposts and go to the next
  • Repeat until all reposted videos are gone

Note: If you have a lot of reposted videos, the process may take a while. TikTok might temporarily block actions if too many happen too fast. In that case, wait about 1 hour and try again.

Tip: When the process finishes, refresh your TikTok profile to confirm that all reposted videos were removed.
